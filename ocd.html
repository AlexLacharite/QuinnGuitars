OCD


Short for Offset Coupé Double, the OCD model is meant to be fully customizable.
With this instrument, you can get obsessive over every detail and make it your own, perfect guitar (or bass).
Choose between 2 different body sizes (15"¼ or 16"¼), any neck joint, hardware, electronics configuration and more.
Your imagination is the limit!
